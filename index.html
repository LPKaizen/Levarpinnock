Shortly after graduating from Sheridan College in 2014 with an advanced diploma in Computer Systems Engineering, I got my first job working IT Support for a small clothing Company called luvali convertibles. After gaining valuable experience, I shifted to Becton Dictonson as an Inventory Control Specialist. During my time there I worked extensively with a SAP-based database, as well as a Lotus Notes database to moniter and manage inventory at a storage and manufacturing facility.

Following this experience, I worked at Premium Retail Services as a Technical Market Trainer, later getting promoted to Territory Sales Manager. I collaborated with Google as a main client to market and sell smart home infrastructure and smartphones. During this period I helped sucessfully launch the first Google Pixel, Google Home, and Chromecast in Canada. After four great years, I was offered a new opportunity with Amazon, managing all national marketing and sales involving anything that had Alexa, or Fire OS installed on it. In 2022, I left Amazon and later joined Motorola where I currently work, actively developing the company's telecommunications business in Canada. During my time here, my team and I have sucessfully grown our market segment share to the third highest in the country, second only to Apple and Samsung.

Since then, I've worked on various side projects and labs, including setting up a virtual windows active directory, building multiple computers, creating virtual cybersecurity digital forensics labs, and building websites from scratch, like the one you're on right now! I've always loved coding and fiddling with computers in general. So much so that I've decided I want to pivot my career towards a more technical role that can better utilize these passions. I could tell you about all of my experience, but seeing is believing. So feel free to check out my projects and tutorials to view some of the stuff I've been working on!
