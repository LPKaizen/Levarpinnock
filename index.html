The purpose of this website is mainly to showcase my web development skills. The site itself was hard-coded from scratch, no Bootstrap or SASS here! (although I can use those when needed), just pure HTML, CSS, and good old JavaScript. I can also code in Python too! Check out my website samples in the tech demos section above!



If you need to build or update your website, or you need a custom built PC solution for your home or business, feel free to reach out to me using the contact information below! For pricing, check out the Services Tab

I graduated from Sheridan College in 2014 with an Advanced Diploma in Computer Systems Engineering. My first job out of school was working as a Data Entry Clerk. I hated it, but loved working from home on my computer, so I stuck it out until a year later when I would start working at Becton Dictonson as an inventory control Specialist. During my time there I got to work extensively with an SAP-based database, as well as an Lotus Notes database to moniter and manage inventory at a storage and manufacturing facility for a one year contract. After completing my contract, I worked at Premium Retail Services as a Technical Market Trainer, later getting promoted to Territory Sales Manager, collaborating directly with Google as a main client to market and sell Smart Home infranstructure and Smartphones. During my time I helped sucessfully launch the first Google Pixel, Google Home, and Chromecast in Canada. After four great years, I was offered a similar position with Amazon doing more or less the same thing, but with anything that had Alexa, or Fire OS installed on it. Then in 2022, I left Amazon and later joined Motorola where I currently work, actively growing market segment share in their telecommunications business. During my short time here so far, my team and I have helped grow our market segment share to the third highest in the country, second only to Apple and Samsung.

Since then, I've worked on various side projects and labs, including setting up a virtual windows active directory, building multiple computers, creating virtual cybersecurity digital forensics labs, and building websites from scratch, like the one you're on right now! I've always love coding and fiddling with computers in general. So much so that I decided I wanted to pivot my career back towards a more technical role that can better utilize these passions. I could tell you about all of my experience, but seeing is believing. So feel free to check out my tech demos and blog in the main menu above to view some of the stuff I've been working on!
